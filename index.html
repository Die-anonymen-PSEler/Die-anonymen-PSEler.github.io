Welcome to RetroMachines!

You’ve been waiting your whole life for that one game, weren’t you? Well wait no longer. RetroMachines is here to SAVE THE DAY. It’s platform game as well as a puzzle game in beautiful old school graphics. But be warned: DANGER OF ADDICTION!

Features:

$ Multiple characters
$ Detailed tutorials
$ 10 handcrafted levels
$ Completely free

Peaked your interest? Get it today and start playing!

Further Information

The game was developed as a project at the German University KIT. Its goal is to explain the way of working of the lambda calculus, which is a central system within in theoretical computer science.

We believe that the project has resulted in a beautiful game for people of all ages.

We would like to thank our institute for giving us the chance to publish the game here in the play store.

Both comments and reviews are greatly appreciated. This does apply to eulogies as well as constructive criticism.
